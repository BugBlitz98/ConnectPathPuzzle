Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 9, 2026

At Connect Path Puzzle, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide a better experience:

  • Usage Data: We may collect information on how the app is accessed and used (e.g., level progress, time spent on levels).
  • Device Information: Basic device model and OS version to ensure compatibility and debug technical issues.
  • Cookies: On our website, we use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our service.
  • Improve game balance and difficulty levels.
  • Monitor the usage of our application to detect technical issues.
  • Provide customer support.

3. Data Storage

Your game progress is primarily stored locally on your device. Any data synced with our servers is encrypted and protected using industry-standard security measures.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services like Google Play Services or Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@connectpath.puzzle
